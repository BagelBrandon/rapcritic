About Rap Critic

About Us

Rap Critic is the dankest website you'll lay your eyes on. We're out here doing the world a favor by criticing rappers and protecting everyone from wack music. Our opinion is fact, and that's a fact. We always follow our moto; it be like that sometimes.

Our Criteria

1-10 Bars Punchlines, double entendres, etc
1-10 Flow Your tone of voice and how well you match beats
1-10 Content What you rap about

Criteria Explained

In order to not be a wack rapper, you have to follow our criteria and improve each of these main areas of rapping to the best of your ability (Can be seen to the left). These sections are vague umbrellas of rap, and each have their own branches and styles. To get your final rapper grade, we average the score of the 3 sections together.
